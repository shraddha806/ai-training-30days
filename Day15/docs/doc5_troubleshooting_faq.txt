Troubleshooting FAQ

Q: The app crashes when I open it. What should I do?
A: Restart your device, check for updates, and reinstall the application if the issue persists.

Q: I forgot my password. How can I reset it?
A: Use the password reset link on the sign-in page and follow the emailed instructions.

Q: Why is the app asking for permission to access files?
A: The app needs access to read and save documents. Grant permission and retry.
